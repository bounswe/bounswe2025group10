# User Agreement

**Last Updated: December 16, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Zero Waste Challenge. This User Agreement ("Agreement") outlines your responsibilities and commitments as a member of our sustainability-focused community. By creating an account and using our platform, you agree to uphold these community standards and guidelines.

## 2. Our Mission

Zero Waste Challenge is dedicated to:
- Promoting environmental awareness and sustainable practices
- Building a supportive community of individuals committed to waste reduction
- Gamifying sustainability to make environmental action engaging and rewarding
- Providing tools and resources for tracking and improving waste reduction efforts

## 3. User Responsibilities

### 3.1 Accurate Information
- Provide truthful and accurate information during registration
- Report waste reduction activities honestly and accurately
- Update your profile information when it changes
- Do not create multiple accounts to manipulate the system

### 3.2 Respectful Conduct
- Treat all community members with respect and courtesy
- Engage in constructive and supportive interactions
- Respect diverse perspectives and backgrounds
- Foster an inclusive and welcoming environment

### 3.3 Content Quality
- Share meaningful content related to sustainability and waste reduction
- Provide helpful tips and experiences based on real practices
- Cite sources when sharing information from external resources
- Avoid posting duplicate, spam, or low-quality content

## 4. Community Guidelines

### 4.1 Acceptable Use
**DO:**
- Share genuine waste reduction tips and experiences
- Encourage and support others in their sustainability journey
- Ask questions and seek advice from the community
- Report content or behavior that violates guidelines
- Give constructive feedback on challenges and features
- Celebrate achievements of community members

**DON'T:**
- Post false or misleading waste reduction claims
- Harass, bully, or intimidate other users
- Share personal information of others without consent
- Promote products or services without authorization
- Post off-topic or irrelevant content
- Engage in arguments or inflammatory discussions

### 4.2 Content Posting Standards

**Posts and Comments:**
- Stay on topic related to sustainability, waste reduction, or environmental issues
- Use clear and respectful language
- Provide context and details for your posts
- Add appropriate images that relate to your content
- Avoid excessive self-promotion

**Tips Sharing:**
- Share practical and actionable advice
- Base tips on real experience or credible sources
- Clearly indicate if something is experimental or theoretical
- Update or correct tips if you discover better information

**Challenge Creation:**
- Design challenges that are safe and achievable
- Provide clear instructions and requirements
- Set realistic goals and timeframes
- Ensure challenges promote genuine sustainability practices

### 4.3 Prohibited Content
You may not post content that:
- Contains hate speech, discrimination, or bigotry
- Promotes violence or harmful activities
- Includes sexually explicit or inappropriate material
- Violates intellectual property rights
- Contains personal attacks or harassment
- Spreads misinformation about environmental issues
- Promotes dangerous or illegal activities
- Includes spam, scams, or phishing attempts

## 5. Waste Tracking Integrity

### 5.1 Honest Reporting
- Record your actual waste reduction activities
- Do not inflate numbers to gain higher rankings
- Use appropriate waste categories (Plastic, Paper, Glass, Metal, Electronic, Oil & Fats, Organic)
- Report amounts in grams accurately

### 5.2 Data Verification
- Understand that waste data is self-reported
- We may review suspicious activity or patterns
- Accounts with fraudulent data may be suspended or terminated
- Leaderboard positions are based on trust and community honor

### 5.3 Learning and Improvement
- Use waste tracking as a tool for personal growth
- Focus on your own progress rather than competition alone
- Share insights from your tracking experience with the community

## 6. Gamification and Fair Play

### 6.1 Points and Badges
- Earn points through legitimate waste reduction activities
- Do not exploit bugs or loopholes in the system
- Report any issues or glitches you discover
- Understand that points and badges have no monetary value

### 6.2 Challenges
- Participate honestly in challenges you join
- Complete challenge requirements genuinely
- Do not create fake or impossible challenges
- Respect the spirit of collaborative environmental action

### 6.3 Leaderboards
- Compete fairly and honestly
- Congratulate and support top performers
- Report suspicious leaderboard activity
- Focus on personal improvement, not just rankings

## 7. Privacy and Data Sharing

### 7.1 Your Data
- You control your profile visibility settings
- You decide what personal information to share
- You can request data export or deletion
- Review our Privacy Policy for detailed information

### 7.2 Respecting Others' Privacy
- Do not share others' personal information
- Do not screenshot or repost private conversations
- Respect privacy settings of other users
- Obtain consent before mentioning others in posts

## 8. Reporting and Moderation

### 8.1 Reporting Violations
If you encounter content or behavior that violates this Agreement:
- Use the report feature on posts, comments, or tips
- Provide specific details about the violation
- Do not engage in vigilante moderation or public shaming
- Trust the moderation team to handle reports appropriately

### 8.2 Moderation Process
- Reports are reviewed by moderators and administrators
- Actions may include content removal, warnings, or account suspension
- Moderation decisions are made to maintain community standards
- Repeated violations may result in permanent account termination

### 8.3 Appeals
- You may appeal moderation decisions
- Contact us with your appeal and explanation
- Appeals are reviewed by senior administrators
- Decisions on appeals are final

## 9. Intellectual Property Rights

### 9.1 Your Content
- You retain ownership of content you create
- You grant us license to display and distribute your content on the platform
- You represent that you have rights to post all content you share
- You are responsible for any copyright or trademark violations

### 9.2 Platform Content
- Respect the intellectual property of Zero Waste Challenge
- Do not copy, reproduce, or redistribute platform content without permission
- Give credit when sharing content from the platform externally

### 9.3 Third-Party Content
- Properly attribute content from external sources
- Respect copyright and fair use principles
- Link to original sources when possible

## 10. Account Management

### 10.1 Account Security
- Keep your password secure and confidential
- Enable two-factor authentication if available
- Log out from shared or public devices
- Notify us immediately of unauthorized access

### 10.2 Account Termination
You may terminate your account at any time:
- Export your data before deletion if desired
- Understand that deletion may be permanent
- Some content (anonymized) may remain for record-keeping

We may terminate your account for:
- Violations of this Agreement or Terms of Service
- Fraudulent activity or manipulation
- Illegal activities
- Safety concerns

## 11. Age Requirements

- Users must be at least 13 years old
- Users between 13-17 should have parental consent
- We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
- Report underage users to help us maintain compliance

## 12. International Use

- The platform is accessible globally
- Respect local laws and regulations
- Be mindful of cultural differences in sustainability practices
- Measurements and units may vary by region

## 13. Changes to Agreement

- We may update this Agreement periodically
- Material changes will be communicated to users
- Continued use after changes indicates acceptance
- Review the "Last Updated" date for recent changes

## 14. Feedback and Suggestions

- We welcome your feedback and suggestions
- Submitted feedback may be used to improve the platform
- We are not obligated to implement suggestions
- You do not gain rights to features based on your suggestions

## 15. Limitation of Warranties

- The platform is provided as a tool for tracking and community engagement
- We do not guarantee specific environmental outcomes
- We are not responsible for actions taken based on community advice
- Consult professionals for specific environmental guidance

## 16. User Conduct Expectations

As a member of Zero Waste Challenge, you commit to:
- Acting in good faith in all platform interactions
- Promoting positive environmental change
- Supporting fellow community members
- Contributing to a healthy and constructive community
- Taking responsibility for your environmental impact
- Being honest, respectful, and ethical

By using Zero Waste Challenge, you acknowledge that you have:
- Read and understood this User Agreement

- Committed to being a positive member of the community
